ABOUT ME

My name is Visay Vang, I am a self-taught developer. I could take a sketch and turn it into a beautiful design with code. I understand that this is a life long learning career because of technology constantly growing and evolving. This is something that I love to do and is up for the challenge.

More of me

My journey into code was an unexpected one. I came from a non-traditional background working as a CNC Machinist. I wanted to further my career as a machinist by learning G codes and came across software development.

I hesitated to pursued at first because of intimidation, but after doing much research it changed my mind. I dove into it and coded my first "Hello World" in JavaScript and was immediately hooked. I knew this is a career I would love to do.

Outside from code, I love to spend time with my family and hangout with friends. I like to watch football, basketball and soccer.
